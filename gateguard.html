Gateguard Guide

METHOD 1: HELP TEAMMATE HOLD LVL 10, HOLD LVL 12 and 14

  • Level 1: 5 Gateguards
  • Level 2: 6 Gateguards. If you sent lvl 1 can push up to 7-1, if didn’t 5-2
  • Level 3: 9 Gateguards
  • Level 4: 11 Gateguards or get an upgraded blue one, push 9/4 taunt units in
  • Level 5: Start upgrading to Heralds. Upgrade middle first.
  • Level 7: Start pushing lumber.
  • Level 8: Build Harbinger on teammate’s side.
  • Level 10: Move 1-2 Heralds to teammate for full sell.
  • Level 12-14: Upgrade Harbingers to Heralds, move build back.

METHOD 2: BIG STACK STRATEGY

If you expect to die on level 10, stack more units early. Build on your teammate’s side all game, pulling waves into your lane.

METHOD 3: SOLO GATEGUARD SPAWN

Try to hold by yourself, upgrade as needed, and push cautiously.
