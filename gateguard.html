METHOD 1: HELP TEAMMATE HOLD LVL 10, HOLD LVL 12 and 14
  • Level 1: 5 Gateguards
  • Level 2: 6 Gateguards. If you sent lvl 1 can push up to 7-1, if didn’t 5-2
  • Level 3: 9 Gateguards
  • Level 4: 11 Gateguards or get an upgraded blue one, push 9/4 taunt units in
  • Level 5: You stop pushing. No more pushing until after 7. You also start upgrading the Gateguards to Heralds slowly but surely.
  • Level 7: After level 7 you start pushing lumber.
  • Level 8: You are still pushing lumber.
  • Level 9: You still build on teammate’s side.
  • Level 10: You already built a lot on your teammate.

If you know you are gonna die with a stack of only wave 8 -> 10 + 2 Heralds on 10, consider stacking more units.
