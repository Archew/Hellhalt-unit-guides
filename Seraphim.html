Seraphim Mechanics & Power System

Understanding Seraphim Power

The Seraphim has a unique mechanic called Power, which ranges from 0 to 50. Raising this Power to 50 is your top priority when playing Seraphim, as it directly affects level absorption efficiency.

  • Power starts at 0/50.
  • Maxing Power (50/50) ensures efficient level scaling.
  • If Power is low, Seraphim will struggle to absorb levels properly.

Seraphim Leveling & Gold Efficiency

The Seraphim has 20 levels, and the cost of leveling follows a structured path:

  • At 50 Power, each level costs 500 gold.
  • Example: If your Seraphim is level 6 (costing 3,000 gold) and you have 50 Power, you'll quickly catch up by spending 5,000 gold, reaching level 10.

How to Increase Power

To increase Seraphim Power, you must sacrifice units that have existed at least one full round.

  • Initially, each sacrificed unit grants 2 Power.
  • Over time, the rate drops to 1 Power per unit.
  • Total sacrifices needed for 50 Power: 35 units.
