Mining Guide

Mining Strategy

This guide will walk you through the basics of playing mines efficiently in the game.

Unopposed Values to Keep in Mind

  • Level 2: Need 280 value
  • Level 3: Need 465 value
  • Level 4: Need 720 value

General Tips

Make sure you TAUNT the mines that need to be focused, especially on Level 4 and 5.

Level-by-Level Strategy

Level 1

  • Push 4/1 (3 wisp + 1 lumber).
  • Build 250 value: 3 medium mines, 1 small mine.

Level 2

Push depends on game speed:

  • Fast Game: Build 5 medium mines. Push 5-2.
  • Slow Game: Go 5-1, upgrade farm first.

Level 3

  • Ensure you have over 465 value.
  • End your build with an ultra mine.

Level 4

  • Push 9/4 (or 9/5 if experienced).
  • Build 1060 value mines strategically.

Level 5

  • Push 9/5 unless expecting a send.
  • Upgrade medium mines to ultras.

Level 6

  • Easy level. Push 9/8 if there’s time.

Level 7

  • Build 7 ultra mines and use small mines strategically.

Level 8

  • Have 9–10 ultra mines.

Level 9

  • Push 9/10 max. 9/11 is risky.
  • Help your teammate if needed.

Level 10

  • You won’t hold alone. Add small delay mines.

Level 11 and Beyond

  • Focus on upgrading to ultra mines while helping your teammate.

Post-Level 17 Strategy

  • Stop playing mines and transition into a supportive role.
